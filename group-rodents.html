Australian Rodents

Australia has over 70 different kinds of Rodent species.
Most of them are rats and mice. They don't usually cause
problems until they get inside your home or office.
Most Rodent species go back over 50 million years.
Infact scientists have presumed that the early chimps evolved
from Rodents. They have been humanities own sanitation squad
helping us to clean up our camps as hunters and gatherers.
So when the black plague hit in the 14th-century.
We were not prepared. The disease spread like wildfire.
The Bubonic plague was carried by fleas on the backs of Rodents.
It remains one of the darkest periods in human history.


Still... we love our furry friends.
Even though they can be big trouble sometimes.
